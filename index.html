About

This very peculiar preconception people have of the ‘Literary and Debate Club’ is this gut feeling that this particular club would be the holy congregation of nerds! It’s true, isn’t it? But trust us when we say this, The Literary and Debate Club of NIT Andhra Pradesh is going to blow past your wildest imagination! We are cool people if we do say so ourselves! You don’t have to believe us, see it for yourself, simply try heading to our Instagram page @lnd_nitandhra and check out our bio! Now that you are back and know how cool and fun we are, let us have a look at what Literary and Debate Club NIT Andhra Pradesh is. We at L&D Club, as it is famously called, believe in dreams. And our belief does not end there we also work together in synchrony, striving for success and give it our all in an attempt to achieve those dreams. Oh look! What’s that bathing in the spotlight, oh it’s our achievements- the highlights!



Highlights
